# Top-Down Scale Approaches for Multiscale GTWR  

This repository provides access to the **real-world dataset** used in:  

> Geniaux, G., Martinez, C. and Soubeyrand, S. (2026) *A Top-Down Scale Approach for Multiscale Geographically
and Temporally Weighted Regression*  

The \texttt{Vaucluse House Prices} dataset (HousePriceVaucluse_44k.Rdata) is derived from France’s open real-estate transactions database, DVF (\url{https://www.data.gouv.fr/fr/datasets/demandes-de-valeurs-foncieres}). It comprises 44{,}236 sales of detached houses (living area $<300$\,m$^2$; parcel area $<5{,}000$\,m$^2$ and parcel area $>100$ m$^2$) in the Vaucluse department between January 2007 and December 2022. 22 attributes are recorded, including sale date, living area (with squared and cubic terms), parcel area (with squared and cubic terms), number of rooms, number and area of outbuildings, and the presence of a swimming pool. Properties are geocoded at the property/parcel level, and timestamps permit aggregation at a monthly temporal resolution.
